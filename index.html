Reminiscence R2M

Reminiscence is a door to immortality: spreads the memories you thrive to share, but understands, protects and worships your deepest secrets. Reminiscence gathers data portraying its community, exposing relations between people and establishing an internet of memories for posterity.

Impact

Reminiscence desires to set up the digital transformation our society is still waiting for. It forges a new Internet of Things approach using the cooperation among people to shape a global pool of experiences, feelings, opinions and memories. We commit to extract insights about cities or even nations: Reminiscence grants access to a worldwide network of anecdotes which intrinsically define humanity.

WHY?

“Verba volant, Scripta manent” we face this pain since the beginning of time: humans have an inclination to share their experiences for future generations, because assembled knowledge is the roadmap to inaugurate the forthcoming era in the most suitable way. However, the humankind direction we should pursue in the future is blurred as we forget our own past and are doomed to repeat the same mistakes again and again. Reminiscence aspires to bring a definitive answer to our human agony.

The growth in fields like Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, together with the maturity reached by Cloud infrastructures, as well as the advancements in Natural Language Processing, inaugurated the era of Big Data Analytics, Internet of Things and Conversational Agents. Those three fields consist in Reminiscence’s playground. Thus, there is no better time to develop an intelligent conversational system in a society already acclimated to interact with pervasive mobile devices.
